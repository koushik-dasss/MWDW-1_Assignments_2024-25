• "Introduction to Algorithms" by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, and Clifford Stein

    Description: Often referred to as the "CLRS book," this is a comprehensive and in-depth resource for learning algorithms. It covers everything from basic data structures to complex algorithms with a focus on rigorous mathematical analysis.

    Why It’s Helpful: It's widely used in university courses and competitive programming circles. The theoretical concepts and proofs provide a deep understanding of algorithms and their efficiencies, making it ideal for interviews and competitive coding.

  • "Algorithms" by Robert Sedgewick and Kevin Wayne

    Description: This book is more practical, with a focus on solving real-world problems using data structures and algorithms. It includes numerous illustrations, code examples, and exercises.

    Why It’s Helpful: It's perfect for beginners who want to see the implementation of algorithms in Java. The exercises help reinforce learning, and it balances theory with practical applications.

  • DSA article

    Description: This article is part of a series designed for beginners, introducing basic data structures (arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues) and foundational algorithms.

    Why It’s Helpful: It's beginner-friendly and easy to follow, offering a step-by-step approach to understanding core DSA concepts with practical examples. GeeksforGeeks also provides multiple solutions to problems, which helps in grasping different ways to solve a problem.

  • LeetCode

    Description: LeetCode is one of the most popular platforms for practicing DSA problems. It offers a vast collection of coding problems, categorized by difficulty (easy, medium, hard) and by specific topics like arrays, graphs, dynamic programming, etc.

    Why It’s Helpful: LeetCode is widely used for preparing for technical interviews. It provides real-time feedback on code submissions and allows you to practice under constraints similar to those in coding interviews.

  • CodeForces

    Description: Codeforces is a competitive programming platform with a strong focus on problem-solving and contests. It offers regular contests, problem sets, and tutorials.

    Why It’s Helpful: For competitive programmers, Codeforces is one of the best resources. It helps develop quick problem-solving skills and offers problems that enhance both DSA and algorithmic thinking under time constraints.
